PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

Income support scheme for all landholding farmer families across the country to assist them in meeting their financial needs.

Key Benefits

  • Financial support of ₹6,000 per year.
  • Transferred directly to the bank account (DBT) in 3 equal installments.
  • No intermediaries, 100% transparent process.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a citizen of India.
  • Must own cultivable land as per land records.
  • Not applicable for institutional land holders or high-income professionals.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land holding papers (Record of Right)
  • Active Bank Account Number
  • Passport size photograph

Application Process

Step 1: Submit Application

Fill out the online form and upload required documents.

Step 2: Document Verification

Local authorities will verify your land records (Takes 7-10 days).

Step 3: Approval & DBT Setup

Once approved, your bank account is linked for Direct Benefit Transfer.

Step 4: Fund Disbursement

First installment of ₹2000 is credited to your account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it applicable for tenant farmers?
No, currently the scheme is only applicable for landholding farmer families whose names are registered in the land records.
How can I check my application status?
You can check your status on the official portal using your Aadhaar number or Bank Account number.
